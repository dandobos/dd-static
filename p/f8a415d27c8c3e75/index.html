7 Days to Choose Your Work
A step-by-step guide to choosing your work
Have you ever felt at peace with who you are, and still unsure where you’re meant to be going?
You’re not performing for anyone or living someone else’s life. What’s missing isn’t character. It’s concrete action.
Before the first lesson arrives tomorrow, let’s review what you shared with me when you took the quiz.
How you described your role
Freelance or contractor
Where you are right now
In transition
 
You’ve had this thought for a while
“Turn my weekend pottery into a real ceramics practice”
Keep that thought in mind. This course will turn it from a “maybe someday idea” into a concrete action plan.
Over the next seven days, this course will each day challenge a popular myth and replace it with an overlooked truth so you can choose the work that makes you feel fully alive.
  • Myth 1: Success comes from discipline.
  • Myth 2: If you’ve invested years into a career, it would be foolish to start something new and squander everything you’ve built.
  • Myth 3: Regret arises from failing to achieve your goals.
  • Myth 4: I need to quit my job so that I can choose my work.
  • Myth 5: Pursue the things that you’re good at.
  • Myth 6: If it can’t be quantified, it doesn’t matter.
  • Myth 7: Real success is financial success.
This course builds on the ideas covered in Choose Your Work. I expect that you might have read Choose Your Work, but if not, that’s fine. You will still receive the full value from the course.
Choose Your Work takes you on an in-depth journey so that you choose the work that you are meant to do. This course aims to fast-track that journey by focusing on practical actions.
